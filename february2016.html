Error 53, Anyone?
Apple is under fire again. This time from those that have updated to the latest version of iOS and can no longer use their phone due to 'error 53'. Apparently not Apple's fault though, as this is part of Apple's protection against unauthorised technicians repairing the finger print home button of the iPhone.

Not too terrible, until you consider the fact that many people appear to have had the phone repaired a few months ago, and have been using it perfectly fine until the recent software update, which is of course going to cause many unhappy customers. We'll have to wait and see what Apple do about this one.
